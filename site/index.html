Britons vote to leave the EU

LEAVE 50.8%
REMAIN 49.2%
  • The leave camp won the day by a slim margin, 1.6% ( 143,472 votes )
  • The places which voted most strongly to leave were: Basildon (64.9%), Swindon (64.8%), Gloucester (64.8%)
  • The places which voted most strongly to remain were: Orkney Islands (64.9%), Waveney (64.8%), South Kesteven (64.5%)
